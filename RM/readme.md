# Secret Warfare

In a world of spies, only the unseen live. As an agent in the Phantom Strike Division, you'll infiltrate, sabotage, and vanish without a trace. With challenging levels and skilled gameplay, every mission is a test of precision and timing. Are you ready to enter the world of warfare and master espionage?

## How to Get Started

Read the intro message, if not your first time it will skip that, then the game will start automaticly. In that menu you can pick levels, change to mobile or computer, restart game or change volume. When you open a level it will give a mission brief that you can skip then the level will start. 

Notice: level one has reports of being complicated, so I'd recomend paper or to skip it.

---

## Controls on a Mobile

Make sure to press the mobile button in the level section menu. There will be a joystick and other buttons if needed. Because of what I coded it on you can not interact with multiple buttons/joystick at the same time. If it says click or press just tap the screen.

---

## Controls on a Computer

Make sure the the computer button in the level section menu. You move by WASD or arrow keys. Space key is used to use items or to fire gun. If level permits the space key and D key to pickup and drop, respectivly.
